A smart card attendance management system is a digital solution that uses smart card to track attendance and used to track the performance of the student. The system facilitates the addition of student records, encompassing crucial information such as semester, name, USN number, address, and parents contact details. It empowers users to efficiently manage academic data by displaying student backlogs, marks, and percentage of attendance.
